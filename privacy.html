Auri AI — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

Auri AI is an Alexa custom skill designed to rewrite messages, explain phrases, and clarify natural language input. The skill does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information of any kind.

No user data, text, voice recordings, identifiers, or metadata is stored by the skill. All processing occurs within Amazon Alexa’s secure environment.

Auri AI does not use external APIs, databases, cookies, third-party analytics, or persistence layers.

The skill is intended for general informational use only and is not intended for children under 13.
