Auri AI — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025

Auri AI is an Alexa skill that provides natural-language assistance such as rewriting messages, explaining phrases, and offering general clarity.

Information We Collect

Auri AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All spoken input is processed securely by Amazon Alexa’s internal systems and is not accessed or retained by Auri AI.

How Your Data Is Used

Your requests are handled in real time by Amazon Alexa. Auri AI receives only the interpreted intent and slot values required to generate a spoken response. No information is stored, saved, logged, or shared by Auri AI.

Third-Party Services

Auri AI does not integrate with any external databases or third-party tracking services. All processing is performed within the Amazon Alexa environment.

Children’s Privacy

Auri AI does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no information is stored, the skill is safe for users of all ages when enabled under parental supervision.
